# Workshop outline — RootRecord 101 (90 minutes)

Use this to teach teammates or partners what RootRecord ships and how pieces connect.

## 0:00–0:10 — Hook

- Story: small business + hazard awareness + optional crypto tooling.
- Show **rootrecord.info** hero and product cards.

## 0:10–0:25 — Product demos (screenshots only)

- Business Manager: dashboard + time tab.
- Weather Manager: alerts card.
- Token Manager: connect wallet screen (no real signing in workshop).
- Account Hub: subscription shortcut.

## 0:25–0:40 — Platform architecture

- Draw three layers: **clients → API → D1**.
- Mention **`api.rootrecord.info`** explicitly.

## 0:40–0:55 — Accounts & billing

- Sign-in → JWT mental model.
- Stripe = money rail; entitlement = feature rail.

## 0:55–1:10 — Solana slice

- Non-custodial vs custodial contrast (use [../10-deep-dives/internal-vs-external-solana.md](../10-deep-dives/internal-vs-external-solana.md)).
- Show **solana.rootrecord.info** as canonical site.

## 1:10–1:25 — Q&A + homework

- Homework: read [../01-introduction/what-is-rootrecord.md](../01-introduction/what-is-rootrecord.md) and [../09-reference/faq.md](../09-reference/faq.md).
