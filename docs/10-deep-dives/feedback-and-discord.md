# Feedback & Discord webhooks

The Worker can POST user feedback to **`DISCORD_FEEDBACK_WEBHOOK_URL`** (`handleFeedbackRoute`).

## Purpose

Fast internal visibility when users hit **Contact** flows—**not** a public chat replacement.

## Privacy

Strip or avoid attaching **full email** unless policy allows; prefer ticket IDs.
